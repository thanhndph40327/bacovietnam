ABOUT BACO VIET NAM


BACO Vietnam Co., Ltd is one of the most reputed manufacturers and exporters of high quality spices, food and agricultural products originated from Vietnam. We have modern and spacious warehouseand factory for processing which ensures the safe storage of products with big quantity so we can meet a varieties of product requirements. Our products are praised for quality both items flavor andappearance by our clients.


Vision

Become the first choice for all Buyers of Spices, Food and Agricultural products form Vietnam.

Staff

Extensive expertise, skilled experienced Professional manaement

Client satisfaction service

Advantages

We specialize in producing high quality tapioca starch, which meets food safety standards in the Vietnamese and international markets and has been tested and certified by SGS.

 

Mission

Quality and Safety are the first priorities. BACO always take care of the first steps from the farm to the processing to make sure well the quality control, food safety, delivery time and supplying capacity.

our product

We produce mainly Cassia Cinnamon, Star Anise, Ginger, Seedlesslime. On other hand, BACO is also the reliable supplier of Pepper, Coffee, Desiccated Coconut, Cashew nuts...
